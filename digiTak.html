How to play DigiTak

Objective:

  • Complete a controlled path from one side of the board to the other (between north and south or east and west)
  • If all tiles have been used or all spaces are occupied the game stops and the player with the most controlled spaces wins

How to play:

  • Both players place a stone on any unoccupied space or move a stone in the cardinal directions during their turn
  • Stones can be placed flat as a path piece
  • Stones can be placed upright as a wall piece (does not contribute to pathing)
  • Capstones do contribute to pathing
  • Already placed stones can be moved onto other already placed stones creating a tower
  • Towers can move any amount from the top and can move up to as many spaces as they are tall, leaving one behind at each space
  • Walls can move onto path pieces, but walls and paths cannot be moved onto a wall
  • Capstones can move onto walls turning them into paths
